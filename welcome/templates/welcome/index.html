This is a gallery of not only art, but also its artists. I encourage you to slow down, look deeply, contemplate, consider, mull over, reckon with, and possibly even go so far as to discuss what you find here. To do so with yourself or with others is your choice.

In this place, you may feel the desire to engage with others by joining a collective with them. This will allow you to chat, collaborate, and draw ideas from others easily. On that note, feel free to imitate, copy, or rip off fellow artists - but please do not steal from them. To copy is an important method of learning, but after all the most valuable style is your own.

Admission is free; please enjoy your time.
